    Tools
  • • Calendar: Schedule events in a calendar, you will receive visual and audio notifications when the time comes.
    Usage eg.
    Schedule an event: Remember me to walk the dog in 10 minutes or Go to the psychiatric tomorrow at 11 am
    To delete events type: delete 02-03-2026
    To delete all events type: delete all
    To show your calendar type: show
  • • Notes: Save thoughts into your notes.
    Usage eg.
    Type a note: Touch grass more often
    To delete notes type: delete note 1
    To delete all notes type: delete all
    To show your notes type: show
  • • Cinema: Search and chat about movies, if you say something like "I want to watch movie xyz" the assistant may send you a third party link to watch the movie.

    • All your calendar and notes data, live in your browser and are not stored anywhere else, unless, you press "share conversation", in that case if you have notes and callendar events in your chat window it will be saved in the server.

    Your notes and calendar events will still be on your browser when you refresh the page.

    The application saves a snippet of the last 25 messages for memory purposes, these memories are deleted 24h after they stop being used.
